Expert Lens Care Tips

Proper maintenance ensures longevity of your premium eyewear

Handle with Clean Hands

Always wash your hands before handling your glasses to avoid transferring oils and dirt to the lenses.

Rinse Before Wiping

Rinse lenses with water first to remove dust particles that could scratch the surface during cleaning.

Use Microfiber Cloths

Only use clean, lint-free microfiber cloths. Paper towels and clothing can scratch lens coatings.
